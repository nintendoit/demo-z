Indian volute

×

Scientific Name

Melo melo (Lightfoot, 1786)

Common Name

Indian volute

Order

Neogastropoda

Family

Volutidae

IUCN Red List Category

Not Evaluated

Distinguishing Morphological Features

Found in intertidal areas in sand. Also in muddy-sand bottoms. Littoral to sublittoral.

Biology

Shell used as decorative items, or as scoops for salt, sugar and flour in the local markets. Also traditionally utilized by the native fishermen to bail out their boats.

Locality collected from

Not Available

Distribution

Indo-West Pacific: Restricted to the Southeast Asian region.
