Red Swimming Crab

×

Scientific Name

Demania baccalipes (Alcock, 1898)

Common Name

Red Swimming Crab

Order

Decapoda

Family

Xanthidae

IUCN Red List Category

Not Evaluated

Distinguishing Morphological Features

Front bilobated with V-shaped median notch. Supra- and infra-orbital margins without spines or teeth. Slightly octagonal in shape. Chelipeds asymmetrical, with the left larger.

Biology

Embryos develop into free-swimming trocophore larvae, succeeded by the bivalve veliger, resembling a miniature clam.

Locality collected from

Not Available

Distribution

Sri Lanka, Malacca Strait, Singapore, Japan, Mumbai, Kolkata, Tamil Nadu coast, Gulf of Mannar.
